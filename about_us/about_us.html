About Us

Cecilia Nyasulu & Friedmann Simfukwe

Cecilia, the founder of the orphanage, completed her diploma in management at the University of Zambia in 2008. After that, she found a job and began working as a project coordinator. But at the same time, she felt the calling of her childhood dream to help other children in need, especially disadvantaged children. After quitting her job, she and her brother Freidman decided to register a foundation as a partner.

Friedmann, a student at the Open University of Zambia decided to register to the foundation as a partner.
