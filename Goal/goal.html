Goals

Encourage children and adults of all ages and abilities to realize their potential and, in the process, help them appreciate their lives and their abilities to make a sustainable difference in their lives, families and communities.

Goal 1: Get all children to believe in themselves and have a clear purpose in life

Goal 2: Value their work and learning and achieve 100% efficient results in all activities and encourage each other to bring out the best in their abilities and talents as this is a key aspect of national growth and well-being development.

Goal 3: Be happy all the time
